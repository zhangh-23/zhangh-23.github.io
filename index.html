About The Game

Play 20 questions with Vertex AI and OpenAI.

Rules

  • Vertex AI or OpenAI picks a secret word
  • User has opportunities to ask up to 20 YES or NO questions
  • AI must answer YES or NO, unless it's a bad question
  • If it's a bad question the machine must refuse to answer, and tell you to take a hike
  • Bad questions count against your total!

Difficulty, hints will be provided by AI after several wrong guesses

  • Easy
  • Medium
  • Hard
